Our Story

A Cut Above
the Rest

Tim's Barber has been a cornerstone of the Parkdale community since 2009. What started as a one-chair shop on Queen West has grown into the neighbourhood's most trusted destination for precision cuts and old-school craft.

We believe a great haircut is about more than hair. It's about slowing down, sitting back, and leaving feeling like the best version of yourself.
